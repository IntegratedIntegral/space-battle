esc to pause/unpause.
w to accelerate in the direction the ship is facing.
s to accelerate in the opposite direction.
a to apply torque in the anti-clockwise direction (turn left).
d to apply torque in the clockwise direction (turn right).
hold shift to apply stability assist, killing your rotation. also allows for slow turning.
x to shoot.
m to toggle the map.
c to dock with the space station. must be within 1500 meters to dock. press c again to undock.
move your mouse while holding LMB to pan the view.
v to center view onto ship.
use mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
use left ctrl + mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out of the map.
